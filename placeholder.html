About Nelli Crafts

At Nelli Crafts, we create stunning jewelry pieces using high-quality epoxy resin and carefully preserved Armenian flowers. Each piece is handcrafted with love and attention to detail, ensuring that every customer receives a unique work of art.

Our jewelry combines modern epoxy techniques with the timeless beauty of natural flowers, creating pieces that are both contemporary and classic.
